Refunds for Digital Products

Refund Policy

This Policy explains when refunds may be granted for purchases of digital training content from Proyecto X.

Last updated: March 2025

Quick summary

Because our content is delivered as instant-access digital products, refunds are only available:

  • if requested within 24 hours of completing the purchase, and
  • if the content has not been fully consumed (for example, the video has not been watched to the end).

1. Digital Products

Proyecto X provides intangible, digital training products, including video modules and related materials (the “Digital Products”). Once payment is confirmed by our payment partner Paddle, access to the content is granted immediately.

2. General Rule – No Refund After 24 Hours

As a general rule, all sales are final. However, we offer a limited refund option subject to the conditions below. Outside of those conditions, refunds are not provided, except where required by applicable law.

3. Limited 24-Hour Refund Window

You may request a refund under the following conditions:

  • The refund request is submitted within 24 hours of the time the purchase was completed and confirmed by Paddle (based on the timestamp shown on your receipt).
  • The relevant Digital Product has not been fully consumed. For video content, this means the video has not been watched in full.

After the 24-hour period has expired, no refund will be granted, even if the content has not been accessed or has only been partially accessed, unless a specific consumer protection law in your jurisdiction requires otherwise.

4. Verification of Content Consumption

Proyecto X reserves the right to verify whether a Digital Product has been consumed before deciding on a refund request. To do this, we may review technical usage data, including but not limited to:

  • Playback progress logs (e.g., percentage of the video watched).
  • Access timestamps and session history.
  • IP addresses and device information used to access the content.

By purchasing a Digital Product, you acknowledge and agree that this type of usage data may be processed and reviewed for the purpose of applying this Refund Policy and preventing abuse.

5. Situations Where Refunds Are Not Granted

Refunds will not be granted in the following situations:

  • The refund request is made after the 24-hour window has expired.
  • The video or other Digital Product has been fully consumed or completed.
  • Buyer’s remorse or a change of mind after purchase.
  • Failure to achieve specific financial or business results.
  • Incompatibility with your device or internet connection issues that are not caused by us.

6. Billing Errors and Duplicate Charges

If you believe you have been charged in error (for example, duplicate billing for the same product), please contact us as soon as possible using the contact details below. We will review your case and, where appropriate, work with Paddle to correct genuine billing errors.

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund within the permitted 24-hour period, please email:

support@proyectox.com (replace with your actual support email).

Your email should include:

  • Your full name and the email used for the purchase.
  • The order ID or receipt from Paddle.
  • The name of the Digital Product you purchased.
  • A brief explanation of your request.

8. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or card issuer for a valid purchase, we reserve the right to suspend or revoke your access to the corresponding Digital Product and to contest the chargeback if we believe it is unfounded or fraudulent.

9. Relationship with Terms of Service

This Refund Policy is part of, and should be read together with, our Terms of Service. In the event of any inconsistency, the Terms of Service and applicable consumer protection laws will prevail.

This Refund Policy is designed to balance fair treatment of customers with the realities of delivering instant-access digital content. It does not replace any mandatory consumer rights that may apply in your jurisdiction. If you are unsure of your legal rights, you should seek independent legal advice.

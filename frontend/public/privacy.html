Privacy Policy

This Policy explains how Proyecto X collects, uses and protects your personal data when you visit our website and purchase our digital training products.

Last updated: March 2025

1. Data Controller

Proyecto X is operated by an individual based in the Dominican Republic. For the purposes of data protection laws, this individual acts as the data controller for personal data processed in connection with the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact information: name, email address and any details you voluntarily provide when contacting us.
  • Account and purchase information: products purchased, amounts paid, currency, dates and access status.
  • Payment information: payment card details are processed by Paddle on our behalf. We do not store your full card number or security code.
  • Technical and usage data: IP address, device and browser type, pages visited, access times and, for digital products, playback progress and access logs (for example, to determine whether a video has been fully viewed).
  • Support communications: messages you send to us, including support requests and refund inquiries.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with access to the Digital Products you purchase.
  • To process and confirm payments via Paddle as Merchant of Record.
  • To manage your account and communicate with you about your purchases.
  • To analyze usage and playback data in order to improve our content and to apply our limited refund policy.
  • To detect, prevent and address fraud, abuse or technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including tax and accounting requirements.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your location, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: to deliver the Digital Products you purchase.
  • Legitimate interests: to improve the Service, enforce our terms, defend legal claims and prevent fraud.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with tax and accounting rules and other applicable laws.
  • Consent: where required by law for specific types of cookies or marketing communications.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Paddle: our payment partner and Merchant of Record, who processes your payments and issues invoices in accordance with their own terms and privacy policy.
  • Service providers: trusted third parties who help us host the website, send transactional emails, provide analytics or maintain security, under appropriate confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Authorities or legal advisors: when required by law, regulation, court order or to defend our legal rights.

6. International Transfers

Due to the global nature of online services, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own, including the Dominican Republic, the United States, the United Kingdom or other jurisdictions where Paddle and our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is protected in line with applicable laws.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, including legal, tax and accounting requirements. After that, data may be anonymized or securely deleted.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as:

  • Accessing the personal data we hold about you.
  • Requesting correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Requesting deletion of your data, where legally permitted.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.
